About us

Ramnad Fish and Chicken(RFC), Inc. is an Indian food processing company, based in Tamil Nadu, India. We are a processor and marketer of fresh chicken, Fish, and Mutton from the East Coastal Region..

We need to make our world a better place and as we are passionate about food, we believe our company could help the customers and society to grow stronger and healthier by providing them with good food.
